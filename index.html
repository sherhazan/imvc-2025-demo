Overview

         

            Our team at Bosch Corporate Research develops end-to-end solutions for robotic manipulation,             leveraging state-of-the-art AI techniques to tackle industrial assembly challenges.             AI4IA enables precise, scalable, and robust robotic assembly, ensuring adaptability to real-world industrial environments.          

 
       
     
       

Perception Utils

               
         
               
  • Object Tracker: Real-time streaming (25 FPS), zero-shot, multi-object tracking based on SAM2.
    •            
  • 6D Pose Estimator: Real-time (10 FPS), zero-shot, based on SAM2 and MegaPose.
    •            
  • Behavior Cloning: Deterministic policy, generalizes across scenarios, robust to lighting, and handles pose variations.
    •            
  • Object Detector: Simple and fast onboarding of new objects, zero-shot, and built on SAM2 and DinoV2.
    •          
       
                       

             
   
           
     
       

Robotic Skills

       
         

We developed a behavior tree of modular robotic skills:

         
               
  • Simple Move: Basic skill for moving the robot to a predefined destination.
    •            
  • Move to Object: Utilizes 6D Pose Estimator to align EEF with the object.
    •            
  • Grasp: Closes the gripper with force-based control to ensure secure grasp.
    •            
  • Screw / Unscrew: Controls bolts with force-based control.
    •            
  • Move Object to: Utilizes 6D Pose Estimator to move the object to a defined pose.
    •            
  • Insertion: Insertion policy controls for smooth insertion, with spiral search reset on failure.
    •
